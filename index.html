Frequently Asked Questions

What is EWPC?

The European Windstorm Prediction Center (EWPC) is a non-official platform providing experimental windstorm forecasts and analyses across Europe.

Are the forecasts official?

No. These outlooks are not official and should not replace forecasts from national meteorological agencies.

How often are outlooks updated?

Outlooks are usually updated several times a week, depending on developing storm systems and available data.

What regions are covered?

EWPC focuses primarily on Europe and surrounding areas affected by extratropical cyclones and strong wind events.

Can I use EWPC data elsewhere?

Yes, but please provide credit to EWPC when sharing or embedding forecasts.
